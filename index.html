CHRISTO CANCER FOUNDATION

Christo Cancer Foundation is a distinguished not-for-profit organization that was established in 2020 with the sole purpose of making a meaningful impact in the fight against cancer. Officially registered in 2023, our foundation is headquartered at No. 124 Amino Kano Crescent, Wuse 2, Abuja, where we tirelessly work towards our vision of ending cancer deaths globally through prevention, early detection, and collaboration.

VISION & MISSION

VISION

Ending cancer deaths globally through prevention, early detection, and collaboration.

At Christo Cancer Foundation, we hold a visionary outlook where the devastating impact of cancer becomes a thing of the past. Our vision is clear and resolute: we aspire to bring an end to cancer-related fatalities on a global scale. We firmly believe that this is attainable through a multifaceted approach that encompasses prevention, early detection, and fostering collaboration among diverse stakeholders.

MISSION

Leading the fight against cancer through awareness, advocacy, consultancy, partnerships, and sponsorships for a lasting impact.

Our mission at Christo Cancer Foundation is to serve as the vanguard in the battle against cancer. We are unwavering in our commitment to this mission, employing a comprehensive strategy that leverages various avenues to drive meaningful change

CORE ACTIVITIES AND INITIATIVES

Fighting cancer since 2020

Conducting extensive awareness campaigns to educate communities about cancer prevention, early detection, and treatment options.

Assisting cancer patients in maintaining a healthy lifestyle during and after treatment. We recognize the importance of holistic wellbeing for cancer patients. Our commitment extends beyond medical care to: Provide guidance on nutrition, physical activity, and mental health to support patients in leading healthier lives. Offer resources and programs aimed at helping patients make positive lifestyle choices that can enhance their overall health and recovery.

Offering financial support and compassionate care to cancer patients and their families. We understand the financial strain that cancer can impose on patients and their loved ones. We strive to: Provide financial assistance to ease the financial burden associated with cancer treatment. Extend emotional and practical support to patients and their families during their cancer journey.

Providing essential support for early detection, diagnosis, treatment, and palliative care for cancer patients. Our foundation is steadfast in its mission to ensure that cancer patients receive the comprehensive care they need from the very beginning of their journey. We strive to: Promote regular cancer screenings and awareness to facilitate early detection. Facilitate access to accurate diagnosis and evidence-based treatment options. Offer palliative care that prioritizes the comfort and well-being of patients facing advanced stage cancer.

Initiating campaigns to raise awareness about cancer. Facilitating the distribution of effective drugs to cancer patients. We believe that awareness and access to essential medications are pivotal in the fight against cancer. We actively: Launch educational campaigns to increase public knowledge about cancer risks, prevention, and early signs. Collaborate with healthcare providers and organizations to ensure that cancer patients have timely access to effective medications and therapies.

Implementing strategies to control and reduce the incidence, morbidity, and mortality rates among cancer patients. Our foundation is dedicated to making a substantial impact on cancer statistics by Developing and promoting preventive strategies that target risk factors and promote early intervention. Advocating for policies and initiatives that improve cancer outcomes and reduce the overall burden of the disease.

Dedicated efforts to enhance the quality of life for individuals affected by cancer. We believe that every individual affected by cancer deserves to live a fulfilling life. Our foundation is committed to: Offering a range of support services, including counseling, support groups, and survivorship programs. Focusing on improving the overall well-being and quality of life for cancer patients and survivors.
